About Email Reminders

What are email reminders?

Email reminders help you stay on top of important tasks and wellness goals. You can set reminders for:

  • Medication schedules
  • Wellness check-ins
  • Appointments
  • Daily habits
  • Health goals

How do they work?

When it's time for your reminder, MomCare will send you an email at your registered email address.

Recurring Reminders

Enable recurring to repeat your reminder daily or weekly at the same time.

Email Configuration

Make sure your email address is correct in your profile and check your spam folder if you don't receive emails.

Tips
  • Set reminders a few minutes before important times
  • Use clear, specific reminder titles
  • Test your email configuration with the test button
  • Recurring reminders repeat at the same scheduled time
  • You can edit or delete reminders anytime
